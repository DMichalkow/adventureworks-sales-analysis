# Profitability Insights

## Overall Profitability

The overall sales revenue amounts to **~109.8 million**, while total product costs reach **~100.47 million**.

This results in a total profit of approximately **~9.37 million**, corresponding to a **profit margin of about 8.53%**.

This relatively moderate margin suggests that although the company generates strong revenue, **product costs consume a significant portion of total sales value**.

---

## Most Profitable Products

The most profitable products are primarily from the **Mountain-200** and **Road-150** product lines.

Examples include:

- **Mountain-200 Black, 42** – profit ~674k  
- **Mountain-200 Black, 38** – profit ~673k  
- **Mountain-200 Black, 46** – profit ~667k  

These products combine **high revenue with relatively strong profit margins**, making them key contributors to overall profitability.

Additionally, several **Road-150 Red** models show particularly strong margins, exceeding **30% in some cases**, indicating that certain road bike models deliver **exceptionally high profitability relative to their cost**.

---

## Regional Profitability

Profitability varies significantly across geographic regions.

The most profitable regions include:

- **Australia** – profit margin ~32.2%  
- **Germany** – profit margin ~21.2%  
- **United Kingdom** – profit margin ~16.1%

These markets generate **healthy profit margins and strong financial performance**.

In contrast, several regions show **very low or even negative profitability**:

- **Central** – negative margin (~-1.8%)
- **Southeast** – negative margin (~-1.9%)
- **Northeast** – negative margin (~-3.9%)

This indicates that sales in these regions may be affected by:

- high operational costs,
- aggressive discounting,
- or unfavorable product mix.

---

## Key Business Implications

The analysis highlights several important strategic insights:

- The company's **overall profit margin is relatively modest (8.5%)**, suggesting that cost management may significantly improve profitability.
- **Mountain-200 product variants** represent the most consistent contributors to profit.
- Some **Road-150 models deliver exceptionally high margins**, making them strategically valuable products.
- Certain regions generate **negative profit margins**, indicating potential operational inefficiencies or pricing issues that may require further investigation.
