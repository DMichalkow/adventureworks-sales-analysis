# Regional Sales Insights

## Regional Revenue Distribution

Sales are unevenly distributed across regions.  
The **Southwest region generates the highest revenue (~27.15M)**, making it the most important market for the company.

The next strongest regions are:

- **Canada (~18.4M)**
- **Northwest (~18.1M)**

These three regions together represent the core revenue drivers.

---

## Regional Revenue Share

The Southwest region alone contributes **over 22% of total company revenue**, making it the dominant market.

Other key contributors include:

- Canada – **~14.9%**
- Northwest – **~14.7%**

Together, these three regions account for **over 50% of total revenue**, indicating a strong geographic concentration of sales.

In contrast, several regions contribute significantly less:

- Germany – **~4.4%**
- Northeast – **~6.3%**
- France – **~6.6%**

This suggests potential opportunities for growth in these markets.

---

## Regional Ranking

Ranking regions by total revenue shows a clear hierarchy of markets:

1. Southwest  
2. Canada  
3. Northwest  
4. Australia  
5. Central  
6. Southeast  
7. United Kingdom  
8. France  
9. Northeast  
10. Germany  

The gap between the top region (Southwest) and the lowest (Germany) is substantial, highlighting differences in regional market performance.

---

## Regional Sales Trends

Revenue trends over time show strong growth in several regions between **2011 and 2013**, followed by a noticeable decline in **2014**.

For example:

- **Australia** experienced significant growth between 2011 and 2013 before dropping in 2014.
- **Canada** shows a similar pattern with strong sales in 2012–2013 and a decrease in 2014.

This pattern may indicate:

- a market cycle
- a change in demand
- or external factors affecting sales in 2014.

Further analysis would be required to determine the cause of this decline.

---

## Business Implications

The analysis suggests several strategic considerations:

- The **Southwest region is the company's key revenue driver** and should remain a primary focus.
- **Canada and Northwest** are also critical markets that significantly contribute to overall revenue.
- Regions such as **Germany, France, and Northeast** may represent **growth opportunities** due to their relatively low revenue share.
- The **drop in sales in 2014 across multiple regions** warrants further investigation to understand potential operational or market changes.
