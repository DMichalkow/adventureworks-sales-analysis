# Sales Overview – Key Insights

## 1. Revenue Trend (2011–2014)

Average monthly revenue is approximately 2.9M, with peak values exceeding 7.2M.  
The overall trend indicates business expansion, particularly in later years.

## 2. MoM Dynamics

Month-over-Month analysis shows high short-term volatility, with strong spikes and sharp corrections.  
This suggests seasonal effects or concentration of large transactions in specific months.

## 3. YoY Growth

Year-over-Year growth exceeds 100% on average, confirming strong long-term expansion.  
However, negative YoY values appear in selected months, indicating periods of temporary slowdown.

## 4. Seasonality

Higher revenue is observed in Q4, suggesting recurring end-of-year demand patterns.

## Business Implications

- Improve demand forecasting to reduce volatility.
- Align inventory and marketing strategies with Q4 peaks.
- Monitor declining YoY periods for structural risks.
