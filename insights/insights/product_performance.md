## Revenue Concentration

Out of 266 products, 63 products (23.7%) generate 80% of total revenue.

This indicates a strong revenue concentration, close to the classic Pareto 80/20 distribution.

## Business Interpretation

- A relatively small product group drives the majority of revenue.
- Revenue stability depends heavily on key products.
- Performance monitoring of top products is critical.

## Strategic Implications

- Prioritize inventory and marketing for top-performing products.
- Monitor revenue trends among key products to detect early risk signals.
- Develop mid-tier products to reduce revenue concentration risk.
